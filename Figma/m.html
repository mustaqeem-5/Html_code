About the Issue

Every Child Deserves To Feel Safe

Child abuse is a pervasive issue affecting communities worldwide. It transcends socioeconomic boundaries, leaving a lasting impact on children's physical, emotional, and mental well-being. At Play it Safe!, we're committed to empowering children and the adults in their lives with the tools to recognize, prevent, and respond to abuse.

A History of Empowering Kids, Protecting Futures.

Play it Safe!® is a child abuse awareness and prevention program developed by The Women's Center of Tarrant County, a leading organization dedicated to addressing violence, crisis, and poverty. With decades of experience, we understand the importance of equipping children, educators, and families with resources that foster safety and confidence.
