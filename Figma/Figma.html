About to Issue

Every Child
Deserves To Feel Safe

Child abuse is a pervasive issue affecting communities worldwide. It transcends socioeconomic boundaries, leaving a lasting impact on the physical, emotional, and mental well-being of children. At Play it Safe!, we're committed to empowering children and the adults in their lives with the tools to recognize, prevent, and respond to abuse.
